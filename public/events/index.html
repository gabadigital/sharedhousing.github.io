Learn how you can buy Single Family Homes, Develop Shared Housing and Tap into Federal and State Funding

date:

Come join Frank and Sherri Candelario, Ph.D.  as they teach you how to purchase, rehab and house people in shared housing.   Learn how to work with local and state agencies, what types of contracts are needed, insurance, and  how to get funded.   This successful team created www.kateshousefoundation.org and built a multi-million dollar real estate  portfolio of rapidly appreciating homes, with mortgages and expenses paid by state and federal funds..  Learn about state and national certification standards.  Sherri is on the Board of Directors of WAQR, the state affiliate of the National Alliance of Recovery Residences.
